COVID-19

We have an answer.

In these unprecedented times our thoughts go out to those affected by the Coronavirus outbreak. Foodkraftz has launched a new meal delivery service focussing on people self-isolating at home or teams of key workers in hospitals, care homes, distribution centres, transport hubs and other workplaces. Our meals can be delivered hot or cold, in recyclable containers with contactless delivery to your doorstop as we take all the necessary precautions to maintain the highest of hygiene standards.
